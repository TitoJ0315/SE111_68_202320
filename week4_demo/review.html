Review of "Scooby-Doo"

Tito Villacorta

January 30, 2023

My review on Scooby-Doo is an amazing movie and just might be the best form of media within the Scooby-Doo IP. The reason I believe this is that it had perfect amount of comedy, action and story telling for a live action adaptation.

My favorite actor within the movie was Matthew Lillard, the way he personified Shaggy it was perfect, to the last minute detail. No matter what role he plays wether it be his role in Scream or his role in Scooby-Doo, Matthew Lillard has such a wide range.

Scooby-Doo Trivia!

  • Trivia #1: Despite the movie poster of the gang being in front of the shadow of the Luna Ghost the villain only appears in the first few minutes of the film. This is because the Luna Ghost was originally intended to be the main antagonist in an early draft of the script.
  • Trivia #2: After filming was completed, Freddie Prinze Jr. had to shave his head, because the bleach ruined his hair.
  • Trivia #3: The band Weezer was asked to make appearances as actors, but rejected the offer.
